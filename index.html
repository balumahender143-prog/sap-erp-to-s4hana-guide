// SAP Landscape

What is SAP S/4HANA?

SAP S/4HANA is the intelligent ERP suite built on the in-memory SAP HANA database — a fundamental re-architecture from classic SAP ERP (ECC), designed for real-time analytics, a simplified data model (the Universal Journal), and AI-embedded processes.

Evolution Timeline

1972 — 1992
SAP R/2
Mainframe-based ERP. Batch processing on IBM MVS. Foundation of modern ERP.
1992 — 2015
SAP R/3 / ECC
Client-server 3-tier architecture. ABAP-based. Modules FI, CO, MM, SD, PP, HR. The dominant global ERP.
2015 — 2020
SAP S/4HANA Launch
In-memory HANA database. Fiori UI. Universal Journal (ACDOCA). On-Premise & Cloud editions.
2020 — 2027+
Intelligent Enterprise
Joule AI copilot, embedded Gen AI, BTP, RISE & GROW with SAP. ECC mainstream support ends 2027.

// Record-to-Report

FI · CO · FSCM · Treasury · Central Finance

The financial heart of S/4HANA. The Universal Journal (table ACDOCA) merges FI and CO into a single source of truth — every accounting line lives in one place, enabling real-time financial close and instant management reporting.

💰
FI — Financial Accounting
Statutory & External Reporting
The legal books of the company. Covers General Ledger (G/L), Accounts Payable (AP), Accounts Receivable (AR), Asset Accounting (AA), and Bank Accounting. Produces balance sheet & P&L per legal requirements (IFRS, GAAP).
⚙ Standard Flow — Record to Report
Business Event→ G/L Posting (FB50)→ Sub-ledger AP/AR→ Reconcile→ Period Close→ Financial Statements
Key tables: ACDOCA (Universal Journal), BKPF/BSEG (legacy view). Sub-ledgers reconcile automatically since all postings flow to one journal.
G/LAP / ARAsset AccountingBankParallel Ledgers
📊
CO — Controlling
Internal Management Accounting
The internal cost view. Cost Center Accounting, Internal Orders, Profitability Analysis (CO-PA), and Product Costing (CO-PC). Helps management understand where money is earned and spent — by product, region, or department.
⚙ Standard Flow — Cost Allocation
Cost Capture→ Cost Center→ Assessment/Distribution→ CO-PA→ Margin Analysis
S/4 change: Account-based CO-PA is now the default, fully integrated in the Universal Journal — no more reconciliation between FI and CO.
Cost CentersCO-PAProduct CostingMargin Analysis
🏦
FSCM — Financial Supply Chain
Credit, Collections & Disputes
Financial Supply Chain Management optimizes working capital: Credit Management (risk scoring), Collections Management (worklists for dunning), Dispute Management (resolving customer deductions), and Cash & Liquidity Management.
⚙ Standard Flow — Collections
Credit Check→ Invoice Overdue→ Collections Worklist→ Dunning→ Dispute / Resolve
Credit MgmtCollectionsDisputesCash Mgmt
💹
TRM — Treasury & Risk
Treasury & Risk Management
Manages cash positions, bank communications, financial instruments (FX, money market, securities), hedge accounting, and market/credit risk. Integrates with SAP Multi-Bank Connectivity and Cash Management.
⚙ Standard Flow — Cash & Trade
Cash Position→ Liquidity Forecast→ Trade FX/Deposit→ Settlement→ Accounting Posting
Cash MgmtFX / HedgingBank ConnectivityRisk Analyzer
🌐
CFIN — Central Finance
Central Finance Hub
A non-disruptive entry to S/4HANA Finance. Replicates financial postings from multiple source systems (ECC, non-SAP) into one central S/4HANA system in real-time via SLT — giving group-wide reporting without a full migration first.
⚙ Standard Flow — Replication
Source ECC/Non-SAP→ SLT Replication→ Mapping (MDG)→ Central S/4 Posting→ Group Reporting
SLTReal-time ReplicationGroup ReportingMDG Mapping

GRC — Governance & Compliance
Governance, Risk & Compliance
Manages access risk (Segregation of Duties), access provisioning, emergency access, process controls, and audit. SAP GRC Access Control prevents one user from having conflicting authorizations (e.g. create vendor + pay vendor).
⚙ Standard Flow — Access Risk
Access Request→ Risk Analysis (SoD)→ Approval Workflow→ Provision Role→ Periodic Review

// Development & Integration Platform

ABAP · UI5 · CPI · BTP

The technical backbone of SAP — from classic ABAP development to modern cloud integration, responsive Fiori apps, and the SAP Business Technology Platform (BTP) that ties extensions, integration, data, and AI together.

⚡
ABAP
Advanced Business Application Programming
SAP's proprietary 4th-generation language for custom reports, enhancements, interfaces, and the S/4HANA "ABAP Cloud" model. Modern ABAP pushes logic down to HANA via CDS Views and exposes services via the RESTful Application Programming model (RAP).
⚙ Standard Flow — RAP Development
CDS Data Model→ Behavior Definition→ Service Definition→ OData Service→ Fiori App
Golden rule in S/4: keep the standard "clean core" — build extensions on BTP or with released APIs, not by modifying SAP code.
RAPCDS ViewsBAPI / BAdIODataAMDP
🎨
SAP UI5 / Fiori
SAP Fiori / SAPUI5
JavaScript framework for responsive, role-based enterprise apps. SAP Fiori is the design language; SAPUI5 is the framework. Apps are deployed to the Fiori Launchpad as tiles, tailored to a user's role rather than a transaction.
⚙ Standard Flow — App Lifecycle
OData Service→ UI5 / Fiori Elements→ Deploy→ Launchpad Tile→ Role Assignment
Two build styles: Fiori Elements (metadata-driven, fast) vs. Freestyle UI5 (full control via MVC). Most standard apps use Fiori Elements templates.
MVCOData V4Fiori ElementsLaunchpad
🔗
SAP CPI / Integration Suite
Cloud Platform Integration
SAP's cloud integration middleware (part of Integration Suite on BTP). Connects S/4HANA with cloud apps (Ariba, SuccessFactors, Concur, Salesforce) using iFlows, adapters, and pre-delivered integration packages.
⚙ Standard Flow — iFlow
Sender Adapter→ Mapping→ Routing/Enrich→ Receiver Adapter→ Target System
iFlowsMessage MappingREST / SOAP / IDocPre-built Packages
🏗️
SAP BTP
Business Technology Platform
SAP's platform-as-a-service that unifies four pillars: Application Development & Automation (CAP, Build), Integration (CPI), Data & Analytics (Datasphere, SAC), and AI (AI Core, Joule). The home of "side-by-side" extensions that keep the S/4 core clean.
⚙ Standard Flow — Side-by-Side Ext.
S/4 Released API→ BTP App (CAP)→ Destination Service→ Consume in Fiori
CAPCloud Foundry / KymaAI CoreClean Core

// Supply Chain

SD · LE · MM · TM · WM · EWM · ATP · IBP

End-to-end supply chain from demand planning and procurement through to delivery — covering sales, materials, warehouse, transport, and the cloud planning suite IBP.

🛍️
SD — Sales & Distribution
Order-to-Cash (OTC)
Manages the full sales cycle: inquiry, quotation, order, delivery, billing, and payment. Configures pricing (condition technique), output, shipping conditions, and credit checks. Tightly linked to LE, MM, and FI.
⚙ Standard Flow — Order to Cash
Inquiry (VA11)→ Quotation (VA21)→ Sales Order (VA01)→ Delivery (VL01N)→ Goods Issue→ Billing (VF01)→ FI Payment
Pricing logic: condition records (VK11) + access sequence → pricing procedure determines net value automatically at order entry.
Order MgmtPricing (VKOA)BillingCredit Mgmt
📦
MM — Materials Management
Procure-to-Pay (P2P)
Covers purchasing, inventory management, and invoice verification. Manages vendor master, purchase orders, goods receipt, MRP-driven requisitions, material valuation, and stock movements (movement types).
⚙ Standard Flow — Procure to Pay
Purchase Req (ME51N)→ RFQ / Source→ PO (ME21N)→ GR (MIGO)→ Invoice (MIRO)→ Payment (F110)
3-way match: PO + GR + Invoice must agree on qty & price before payment is released.
MRPInventoryMIGO / MIROValuation
🚛
TM — Transportation Mgmt
Freight & Transport Planning
Plans, optimizes, and executes freight orders. Carrier selection & tendering, route optimization, freight cost settlement, and track & trace. Integrates with LE for delivery and FI for cost posting.
⚙ Standard Flow — Outbound Transport
Sales Order / Delivery→ Freight Unit→ Carrier Tendering→ Freight Order→ Track & POD→ Cost Settlement
Freight OrdersCarrier MgmtRoute OptimizeSettlement
🏬
WM / EWM — Warehouse
Warehouse Management & EWM
Classic WM (LE-WM) handles bin-level stock with transfer orders. EWM is the advanced successor: storage control, wave/pick management, labor management, RF/voice picking, slotting, and yard management. EWM is the strategic choice in S/4HANA.
⚙ Standard Flow — Inbound (EWM)
ASN / Inbound Delivery→ Goods Receipt→ Putaway Task→ RF Confirm to Bin→ Stock Updated
Storage BinsPick RoutesRF ScanningLabor MgmtSlotting

ATP — Availability Check
Available-to-Promise (aATP)
Real-time check of material availability at order entry. S/4HANA Advanced ATP (aATP) adds multi-step Product Availability Check, Backorder Processing (BOP) with prioritization, Product Allocation, and Release for Delivery.
⚙ Standard Flow — aATP
Order Entry→ Check Stock + Receipts→ Product Allocation→ Confirm Date/Qty→ Backorder Reprioritize
aATPBackorder (BOP)AllocationRelease for Delivery

LE — Logistics Execution
Shipping & Delivery Execution
The bridge between SD orders and physical shipment. Handles outbound/inbound deliveries, picking, packing (handling units), goods issue, and returns. Triggers warehouse tasks in WM/EWM and shipment docs in TM.
⚙ Standard Flow — Shipping
Outbound Delivery (VL01N)→ Pick→ Pack (HU)→ Post Goods Issue→ Carrier Handoff

Supply Chain Planning (Cloud)
SAP's cloud planning suite (successor to APO). Modules: Demand, Supply & Response, Inventory Optimization, S&OP, and Control Tower. Runs on HANA with an Excel-based UI and real-time scenario simulation.

// Production & Engineering

PP · QM · PM · PS · CS

From production planning and quality through to maintenance, project systems, and customer service — the make, maintain, and serve side of S/4HANA.

🏭
PP — Production Planning
Plan-to-Produce
Manages BOMs, routings, work centers, MRP, capacity planning, and shop-floor control. S/4HANA's MRP Live (MD01N) runs planning in-memory in real time, replacing overnight batch MRP. Supports discrete, process, and repetitive manufacturing.
⚙ Standard Flow — Plan to Produce
Demand / PIR→ MRP Live→ Planned Order→ Production Order (CO01)→ Confirm (CO11)→ GR to Stock→ Order Settlement
Master data: Material Master + BOM (CS01) + Routing (CA01) + Work Center (CR01) = the recipe MRP uses to plan.
MRP LiveBOM / RoutingCapacity PlanningDiscrete / Process
✅
QM — Quality Management
Quality Inspection & Control
Quality checks at goods receipt, in-process, and final inspection. Manages inspection lots, sampling, results recording, usage decisions, defect/notification handling, quality certificates, and QM in procurement (vendor quality).
⚙ Standard Flow — Inspection
Trigger (GR/Prod)→ Inspection Lot→ Record Results (QE51N)→ Usage Decision (QA11)→ Stock Posting
Inspection LotsUsage DecisionDefectsQuality Certs
🔧
PM / EAM — Plant Maintenance
Asset & Maintenance Management
Manages technical objects (functional locations, equipment), breakdown and preventive maintenance, maintenance plans, and spare-parts integration with MM. Now part of SAP EAM (Enterprise Asset Management), with predictive maintenance via IoT.
⚙ Standard Flow — Breakdown Maintenance
Notification (IW21)→ Maint. Order (IW31)→ Plan & Issue Parts→ Execute & Confirm→ Settle to Cost Center
Equipment MasterWork OrdersPreventive PlansPredictive (IoT)

PS — Project System
Project Management & Costing
Plans and controls complex projects — capital investments, make-to-order engineering, R&D. Uses the Work Breakdown Structure (WBS), networks, activities, milestones, and budget control. Integrates costs from MM, PP, HR, and CO.
⚙ Standard Flow — Project Lifecycle
Project Def (CJ20N)→ WBS + Network→ Budget & Release→ Execute (costs post)→ Settlement to Asset/CO
WBSNetworksBudgetingResult Analysis

Service Management
Manages after-sales service: service notifications, service orders, warranties, repairs (in-house & field), spare parts, and service contracts with periodic billing. Bridges SD, PM, and MM. In S/4 it evolves into SAP Service / Service Cloud.

ECC → S/4HANA Migration

SAP ECC mainstream support ends 2027 (extended to 2030 with premium). Three paths exist: Greenfield (new), Brownfield (conversion), or Bluefield (selective).

🌱
Greenfield
New Implementation
Start fresh on S/4HANA, redesigned to SAP Best Practices. Clean slate — only master data and open items migrate. Highest value & process reengineering, longest timeline.
Best PracticesSAP Activate12-24 months
🟤
Brownfield
System Conversion
Convert the existing ECC system in-place using SUM/DMO. All historical data and most config retained. Faster and lower disruption, but legacy customizations carry over.
SUM / DMOHistorical Data6-18 months
🔵
Bluefield / Selective
Selective Data Migration
Hybrid — selectively migrate chosen company codes, entities, or time periods using tools like SNP. Combines a clean redesign with retained relevant data. Flexible but complex.
SNP BluefieldSelectiveHybrid
